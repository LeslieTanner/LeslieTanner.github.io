Situation:

C/1-502 In has been in country for over ~2 months and has had several successful offensive against the Atropian forces; US Forces are now firmly in control of Muksha, Agdash, Vetlia, Erdabil, and Goychay Provinces. The enemy in the STRIKE BDE AOR has been pushed southwest of the Platt River. However, Atropian forces have recovered from the initial US offensive and are establishing a hasty defense in depth on the west side of the Platt River in our BN AOR. An Atropian Company(+) has been identified moving into defensive positions on elevated terrain to the west of the Platt River vicinity the Bear Mountain Bridge.

The Battalion S-2 believes the enemy will defend in depth to destroy US Forces before crossing the Bear Mountain Bridge and destroying the east-west running avenue over the Platt River. The villages near the Bear Mountain Bridge are sympathetic to Majnun Farhad and likely have provided 4x4 vehicles to the enemy. The vehicles will be utilized to move forces along trails to their defensive positions and subsequently used to transport Atropian forces during their withdrawal across the Bear Mountain Bridge. We believe the Enemy intends to inflict as many casualties on US Forces as possible before withdrawing to the east in an attempt to dishearten the American People who will in turn call US Forces to be withdrawn from Atropia. With the Bear Mountain Bridge in jeopardy, TALON 6 indicates it is imperative that we conduct destroy the enemy before they are allowed to cross the Platt River and destroy this key infrastructure.

Observation and Fields of Fire- Observation and fields of fire will be limited based on the prevailing vegetation. Hardball roads will be cleared and thus will act as linear danger areas with high observation and visibility.

Cover and Concealment- Cover: Use of natural terrain features (thick trees, rocks) will offer our only cover. Concealment: The trees and ridges will cover and conceal most movement formations. Traveling in the wood line will provide cover and concealment out to 25 meters. In some areas, the low vegetation will provide very little to no cover and limited concealment. Periods of limited visibility provide the best time for concealed movement. We can expect actions on the objective to occur within a close range due to the heavily forested area and hilly terrain.
