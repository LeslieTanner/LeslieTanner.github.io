Concept of the Operation. The purpose of this operation is to allow 3-15 IN to pass onto OBJ PUMA. We will accomplish this by conducting a frontal attack. Decisive to this operation is seizing a foothold on OBJ BEARS. We will assume risk by utilizing RTE FORD en route to OBJ BEARS. We will mitigate this risk by utilizing RTE clearance and deception fires. The purpose of fires is to destroy ADA assets and to suppress enemy on objectives; on order destroy enemy battle positions.

Scheme of Movement and Maneuver.

PHASE I: MOVEMENT and INFIL- This phase begins with SP of GAC1 Serial 1 from FOB ANVIL. This phase ends when 3-7 INFIL to ATK POS BALER is complete. During this phase the enemy will be establishing a large scale defense out to the 15RVQ 99 easting. The DO during this phase will depart FOB ANVIL in GAC 1 Serial 2 at H-0:50. SO1 will depart in GAC 1 Serial 1 at H-1. SO2 will depart in GAC 1 Serial 3 at H-0:40. The TF Reserve will depart in GAC 1 Serial 2. Priority of fires for this phase is SO1, DO and SO2. The decision point during this phase is: If the ZSU is identified in BATOOR and is not destroyed, do we continue breach?

PHASE II: SECURE FOOTHOLD, ASSAULT, and CLEAR- This phase begins when isolation and breach on OBJ BEARS NORTH is initiated. This phase ends when OBJ BEARS SOUTH is secure. During this phase the enemy will be in a strongpoint defense on OBJ BEARS. The DO during this phase will follow and support SO2. SO2 will Seize OBJ BEARS NORTH (MARGHOZ). SO1 will seize OBJ BEARS SOUTH (BATOOR) after SO2 seizes OBJ BEARS NORTH. Priorities of Planning for the TF Reserve are reinforcing OBJ BEARS SOUTH and attack by fire.

PHASE III: SEIZE OBJ BRUINS- This phase begins when SO1 conducts passage of lines with the DO onto OBJ BRUINS. This phase ends when OBJ BRUINS is seized. During this phase the enemy will utilize recon elements from SANGARI to attempt to disrupt movement westward along RTE FORD. They will attempt to prevent the seizure of OBJ BRUINS. The DO will seize OBJ BRUINS. SO1 will consolidate and reorganize on OBJ BEARS SOUTH and prepare for follow on operations. SO2 will follow and support the DO, stage in the west of OBJ BEARS SOUTH and if needed, seize the BDE HLZ.
