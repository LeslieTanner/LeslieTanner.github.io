1. (U) Situation. On 14OCT14, the armed forces of Ariana invaded eastward and took control of the Republic of Atropia (ROA), with the goal of installing a new client government in ROA that will support Arianian ideologies and economic interests. The Arianian Army (AA), with support of the Arianian Air Force (AAF), attacked with three of the AAs Operational Strategic Commands (OSC)s, and seized the ROA capital of Baku, securing access to Atropias rich hydrocarbon resources, refineries, and pipelines. Arianian intends to use its newfound wealth and territory to establish itself as a regional power. Reporting indicates that the AA has begun a campaign targeting unsympathetic Atropian political leaders and is conducting ethnic cleansing campaigns against non-Persian ethnic groups. The Arianian occupation is likely to more widely destabilize the region.

c. (U) Enemy Forces.

(1) (U) Enumeration.

(a) (U) State Actors.

1. (U) Arianian Army (AA). The AA is a functional and capable Tier II ground force, equipped with a full complement of Soviet-era equipment from the OSC level down to squad and fire team level. The 181 Brigade Tactical Group (BTG) is the main AA unit operating in 21ID AO BLACKJACK and is primarily composed of truck motorized infantry task-organized with armor.

2. (U) Arianian Special-Purpose Forces (SPF). Arianian SPF are the unconventional warfare element of the AA, capable of conducting direct action (DA), reconnaissance and assassination missions. SPF can employ IEDs, HME, mortars, MANPADs, and can conduct limited counter-mobility operations. They are trained in the use of chemical weapons and may have some capability to disrupt GPS, radio, television, and internet communications through jamming. They are able to utilize SIGINT and Full Motion Video (FMV) collection assets.

3. (U) Arianian Air Force (AAF). The AAF is a functional and capable Tier II Air Force, though we assess AAF pilots are not as capable as USAF pilots and would likely lose air parity after 10 days of sustained aerial combat. The AAF is capable of conducting ground attack, air intercept, transport, Integrated Air Defense (IAD), and intelligence collection through SIGINT, FMV, and Electronic Warfare.

(b) (U) Non-State Actors. Additional threats operating in 21ID AO BLACKJACK include several asymmetric and unconventional groups which may impact operations in AO PENNSYLVANIA.

1. (U) South Atropia Peoples Army (SAPA). SAPA is an ethnic Persian, Atropian insurgent movement with cultural and financial ties to Ariana, which for years has provided it with political and military state sponsorship and support. We assess SAPA leaders are seeking to establish control over Persian enclaves and eventually form them into a separate nation state. We do not know the size of SAPAs total membership, but it is strongest in areas of high Persian ethnicity such as AO PENNSYLVANIA. Since the AA invasion, the AA has been working to integrate SAPA in its operation. Recent reporting indicates SAPA have been operating forward of AA forces in a screen.

2. (U) The Guardians of the Jurists and Godly Martyrs Brigade (Faqih) is a regional Islamist organization supported by various Islamic charities and AQ. Their end goal is the establishment of an Islamic caliphate in the region.

3. (U) The Baqquani Crime Family (BCF). The BCF is active in AO PENNYSLVANIA. BCF operations include trafficking arms, munitions, and drugs. BCF is primarily motivated by money and power.

(2) (U) Road to War: Arianian Invasion of the ROA (14-18OCT14). On 14 October 2014 three Operations Strategic Commands (OSC) invaded the ROA. The invasion began with aircraft attacking Atropian airfields and key command and control nodes including the Atropian Air Defense HQ at BAKU. These attacks destroyed most of ROAs fixed-wing aircraft and much of its air defense capability. During the subsequent five day attack ROAA resistance was quickly overwhelmed by AA T-90 and T-72 equipped Division Tactical Groups (DTG). Previously-infiltrated Arianian SPF executed a series of attacks against mobilizing Atropian forces within the country resulting in key ROA military and political leadership being neutralized. SPF simultaneously seized an unknown number of energy platforms in the Caspian Sea. On 18 October 2014 the AA 96 Airborne Division supported by SPF commandos assaulted into the ROA capital of BAKU using fixed-wing transport aircraft. These forces quickly seized Baku International Airport (15RYP7621), the Baku Naval Air Station, key government buildings, communications nodes, and hydrocarbon infrastructure.

(3) (U) Current Tactical Situation (17NOV14). Currently, committed Arianian forces in Atropia are estimated to have retained 60 to 75 percent of their overall combat power and equipment readiness. J2 assesses that if unhindered, Arianian forces could resupply, rearm and refit to reach 90 % readiness by mid-December 2014. Elements of 181 BTG and 182 BTG are the primary threats in AO PENNSYLVANIA, and are staging defensive operations in the vicinity of De Ridder (OBJ PATTON), a town of approx. 100,000 people. Control of OBJ PATTON is critical because De Ridder lies on the northern flank of 182 BTG operating to the south of AO BLACKJACK, and because one squadron of the AAs 310th Combat Aviation BDE (MI-24 HIND) is using an airfield west of the city (15R VQ67691136) as a forward airfield to support operations in De Ridder and Leesville.
