1. Situation:

a: Enemy Forces: Battalion S-2 confirms reports of enemy movement and offensive operations at OBJ COLTS. Numerous enemy reconnaissance elements have been observed moving through and possibly coordinating future attacks in AO Riley.

Composition & Disposition: Ahuristan military units are operating in squad size elements and possess AK-47s, PKMs, RPGs, and hand grenades.

Capabilities: The enemy is capable of conducting a small arms sustained fight with ammo from supply caches. The enemy may have indirect fire support by artillery and mortars. Attacking elements are capable of being reinforced within 10-20 minutes by multiple squad-sized security elements.

Most Probable COA: The enemy places squad sized security teams along their routes and conducts regular patrols to identify US Forces. If attacked, the enemy will return fire in order to cover their withdrawal, contact their higher headquarters, and develop a plan to conduct a counter-attack on US Forces.

Most Dangerous COA: The enemy uses IDF to harass and draw US Forces into near ambush. Then the enemy deploys reinforcements to close in and destroy US Forces.
